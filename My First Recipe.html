Blue berry French Toast

Ingredients

    1-(24-inch)Bagutte

    6 Large Eggs

    3 Cup Milks

    1/2 Tea Spoon Nutmeg

    1 Teaspoon Vanilla

    1 Cup Packed Brown Sugar

    1/4cup(1/2 Stick) Umsalted Butter

    1/4 Teaspoon Salt

    Two cup BLUE BERRIES(about 12 ounces)

Now Make it

  1. Grease 13/9* baking dish
  2. Out enough 1-inch slices of bread to form a single layer in the baking dish.
  3. #Arrange slices in dish.
  4. In large bowlwhisk together eggs,milk,nutmeg,vanilla and 3/4 cup brown sugar and pour evenly over bread.
  5. Chill mixture,covered,until all liquid is absorbed by bread,at least 8 hours or upto one day.
  6. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F*
  7. sprinkle Blue berries evenky over bread mixture.
  8. Cut 1/2 stick butter into pieces,and in a small saucepan heat with remaining 1/4 cup brown sugar,stirring,until butter is melted.
  9. Drizzle butter mixture over bread and bake mixture for 20 minutes.

Now Ready To Eat It...
